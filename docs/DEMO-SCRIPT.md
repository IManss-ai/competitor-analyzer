# Rivalscope — 60-Second Mentor Demo Script

**The story:** a cold signup turns into "here's your business, here's your real
competitors, and here's exactly where you beat each one" — in minutes, live.

---

## Before you start (2 min, do it once)
- Open **rivalscope.dev** in a clean browser tab (logged out). Use a normal-size
  laptop window or phone — both look right.
- Have a **second tab** ready on the pre-warmed account as a safety net (below).
- If on shaky wifi, do the **pre-warmed path** instead of the live signup.

---

## PATH A — The live "magic moment" (the wow, ~60s)
Do this if the connection is solid. It's the strongest version because they watch
it happen.

1. **Land on rivalscope.dev.** One line: *"Rivalscope watches your competitors and
   tells your sales team exactly how to win."* (Don't read the page — let them see
   the clean landing for 3 seconds.)
2. **Click "Start free" → sign up** with a fresh email (e.g. `demo+<today>@rivalscope.dev`,
   any password 8+ chars). You land on the dashboard with the onboarding modal.
3. **"Add your website" step → type your real business URL.** For the meta moment,
   use **`rivalscope.dev`** itself. Click **Analyze my business**.
   - Say while it runs: *"It's reading our own site right now — no setup, no SDK."*
4. **Review screen appears (~20-40s):** point at the two halves —
   - *"It read our business: automated competitive intelligence for sales teams."*
   - *"And it found our real competitors — Crayon, Klue, AlphaSense, Similarweb —
     not random, the actual players in our space."*
5. **Click "Start tracking."** It adds them and generates the first analysis.
6. **Open a competitor → the head-to-head:** *"This is the payoff — where we win,
   where we're exposed, and the exact plays to beat them. And see these 'inferred'
   tags? We never fabricate — every point shows its evidence. That honesty is the
   whole point of a CI tool."*

**If discovery is slow or thin live:** don't wait — say *"here's one I ran earlier"*
and switch to Path B tab.

---

## PATH B — Pre-warmed account (instant, zero risk)
Everything is already generated. Loads in <2s. Use as the safety net OR the main
demo if you want it bulletproof.

- **Login:** `demo-rivalscope@rivalscope.dev` / `demo-rivalscope-123456`
- It opens straight to a populated dashboard with **Rivalscope's own profile** +
  **Klue and Crayon** already tracked, both with a full head-to-head cached.
- **Click Klue → head-to-head:**
  > "Rivalscope is more agile and targeted for SMBs; Klue is more enterprise."
  Wins: automated battle cards, broader signal tracking, SMB focus.
  Plays: highlight automation, target SMBs Klue overlooks, lean on review+hiring signals.
- **Click Crayon → head-to-head:**
  > "Rivalscope offers more automated, structured battle cards and broader review
  > intelligence; Crayon's lack of recent changes suggests a slower pace."
- Flip between the two to show it's per-competitor, not generic.

---

## The revenue beat (have this ready — it's the kick-out criterion)
- *"And we monetize today — real Polar checkout, live."* On the landing, click
  **Pricing → Start free trial** to show the real `polar.sh` checkout page (SaaS
  $49 / Local $19). Production billing is live; this takes real cards.
- (Don't complete a charge on stage unless you mean to. Showing the working
  checkout is enough; one real purchase beforehand = "we have revenue.")

---

## One-liners to have in your pocket
- *"Cold signup to a competitive battle card in under two minutes."*
- *"It finds your real competitors, not a generic list — and proves its work."*
- *"Same engine that profiles you profiles them — that's the moat."*
- If asked about local businesses: *"Online/SaaS gets auto-discovery today; local
  businesses profile + add competitors manually — geo-discovery is next."*

## If something breaks
- Onboarding slow → Path B.
- A competitor page won't scrape → it degrades gracefully, just pick another.
- Anything 500s → refresh; the dashboard + head-to-head are cached and resilient.
